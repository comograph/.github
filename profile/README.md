## What Comograph does

Comograph replaces resume screening with **demonstrated engineering work**. Engineers take on real challenges from employer stacks, submit a public GitHub repo and a Loom walkthrough, and receive transparent scores within 72 hours. Employers get ranked shortlists, side-by-side comparisons, and reputation profiles — not another pile of CVs.

## How it works

1. **Engineers build proof** — Take on real challenges, ship repos and walkthroughs, and earn scored capability that lives on a public profile at `comograph.com/{slug}`.
2. **AI scores what matters** — Delivery, architecture, documentation, and communication are evaluated automatically and published to engineer profiles.
3. **Employers hire on evidence** — Browse ranked talent, shortlist proven engineers, and skip resume screening.

## For engineers

- **Real proof-of-work** — No whiteboard puzzles. Ship deployable solutions to problems that mirror actual engineering work.
- **Portable reputation profiles** — A scored history with challenge timeline, skill breakdown, and consistency streak you can share anywhere.
- **Per-challenge leaderboards** — See where you rank and how your submissions compare across technical dimensions.
- **Early-career wedge** — Bootcamp grads and junior engineers prove capability beyond pedigree alone.

## For employers

- **Post real challenges** — Define backend APIs, fullstack features, or DevOps pipelines from your actual stack. Set difficulty, deadline, and deliverables.
- **Review proof, not resumes** — Track active challenges, monitor submissions, and move candidates through a pipeline built on evidence.
- **Ranked shortlists** — Compare scores side-by-side and drill into individual engineer profiles before you reach out.
- **Anonymous or branded** — Post challenges confidentially or under your company name. You control what candidates see.
- **72-hour scoring SLA** — Submissions are scored fast so your pipeline moves at the speed of delivery, not calendar scheduling.

## Transparent scoring

AI evaluates what engineers actually build — repositories, documentation, architecture notes, and Loom videos. Capability scores measure demonstrated skill, not popularity or employer bias. Hiring decisions stay with employers; they never control reputation scores.

**Per-submission rubric**

| Dimension | Weight |
| --- | --- |
| Technical | 40% |
| Problem Solving | 20% |
| Documentation | 15% |
| Architecture | 15% |
| Communication | 10% |

**Reputation capability framework**

| Component | Weight |
| --- | --- |
| Challenge Performance | 50% |
| Challenge Difficulty | 20% |
| Consistency | 15% |
| Employer Demand Signals | 15% |

Demand signals — profile views, shortlists, interviews, offers, and hires — accumulate over time to reflect real market interest without letting employers manipulate scores.

## Global talent

Africa's tech talent pool is growing fast — skilled, remote-ready, and hungry to prove capability. Comograph gives early-career engineers a way to demonstrate what they can do, and gives employers a scored shortlist without geographic guesswork.
